ALLU ARJUN

Allu Arjun was born on April 8. He is one of the most loved actors in the Telugu film industry. Known for his stylish looks, energetic dance moves, and powerful acting, he earned the title “Stylish Star.” He gained fame with the film Arya, which made him a youth icon. Later, movies like Bunny, Desamuduru, Julayi, and Ala Vaikunthapurramuloo made him even more popular. In 2021, he starred in Pushpa: The Rise, which became a massive hit across India. The dialogue “Thaggede Le!” went viral and became iconic. Allu Arjun's fans admire his dedication, hard work, and humility. He continues to win hearts with each performance. From Arya to Pushpa, Allu Arjun remains one of India’s most stylish and talented actors. 🎬
